About

A modern academic profile built on research quality and real-world impact.

Dr. Bhadram Kalyan Chekraverthy is an Assistant Professor with strong experience in analytical, bio-analytical, nutritional, phytopharmaceutical, and nutraceutical research. His work spans scientific investigation, academic coordination, student mentorship, research writing, and laboratory documentation.

He focuses on improving research accuracy, strengthening teaching excellence, developing robust analytical protocols, and translating complex scientific work into publications, chapters, conferences, and funded initiatives.
